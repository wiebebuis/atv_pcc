In this digital_garden I share my Obsidian vault made for the knowledge domains related to the music theory education of the bachelor of classical music at the Prince Claus Conservatoire in Groningen (NL). Related to it are the sites [https://skole.nl](https://skole.nl/) and [https://h5p.skole.nl](https://h5p.skole.nl/). The idea behind this repository is to create and share an evolving semantic network. Inspired by (for me) two important writers: John D. Novak (for his views on learning, creating and using knowledge) and Sönke Ahrens (for his views on "taking smart notes" inspired by the Zettelkasten method/tool of Niklas Luhmann). A big thank you to Jacky Zhao ([https://github.com/jackyzha0/quartz](https://github.com/jackyzha0/quartz)) who created Quartz and Nicole van der Hoeven for her clear YT-video ([https://youtu.be/6s6DT1yN4dw?si=w8NzsL9tVgaJM_F](https://youtu.be/6s6DT1yN4dw?si=w8NzsL9tVgaJM_F)) on how to get going with Obsidian, Quartz and Github. ...
